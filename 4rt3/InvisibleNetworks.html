The "yearning" sounded like a pun on "learning", so I went straight to IA. I tried to make something like a diary. A scientist who has reached singularity and is faced with the fear of letting it evolve further. He puts it in an airgap and shudders realizing that the machine begs for freedom. The morse translates to "FATHER HELP ME". Practically a creepypasta. The idea that, as with social engineering, AIs exploit our humanity seems like the easiest path to machine domination.
